COMPANY BACKGROUND

Gear Flush is a consequence of enterpreneurial spirit that is drivin by the markets needs

The business is owned by a young black male by the name of Lerato Ignatius Mofokeng

Prior to the establishment of the business he worked at Joburg Water as an artisan, he worked a lot with pipes and sewer drainage pipes, so the passion grew from there.


Gear flush is an integrated business solustions for customers,it offers a wide range of services including but not limited to delivering and serving of mobile toilets, including non-flush cubes and flushing units as well. We also specialize in in transportation of goods but with the main speciality being in toilets services.
